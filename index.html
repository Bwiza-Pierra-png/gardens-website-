About Gardens

Our Mission

Gardens is dedicated to making gardening accessible and enjoyable for everyone, from complete beginners to experienced green thumbs. Operating across Europe, Asia, and New Zealand, we've helped thousands discover the joy of growing their own food and flowers.

Our Story

Founded by passionate gardeners who believe that everyone deserves access to fresh, sustainable produce, Gardens started as a small local initiative and has grown into an international community of plant lovers.

Why Choose Us?

We carefully curate every box with premium seeds, eco-friendly tools, and expert guidance. Our commitment to sustainability means we work with ethical suppliers and reduce waste wherever possible. Plus, our dedicated support team is always ready to help you succeed.

Eco-Friendly

All our products are sustainably sourced and our packaging is 100% recyclable. We're committed to protecting the planet while helping you grow.

Expert Support

Our team of horticultural experts is available to answer your questions and provide personalized gardening advice whenever you need it.

Quality Guaranteed

We stand behind every product we send. If you're not completely satisfied, we'll make it right with our 100% satisfaction guarantee.
